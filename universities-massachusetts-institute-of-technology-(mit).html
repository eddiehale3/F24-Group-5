Overview

The Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT) is a private research university located in Cambridge, Massachusetts. Founded in 1861, it is renowned for its contributions to science and technology and is consistently ranked among the worldâ€™s top universities.

Location

MIT is located at 77 Massachusetts Avenue, Cambridge, MA 02139, USA. The campus spans 168 acres along the Charles River in Cambridge.

Academic Programs

MIT offers a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs across five schools:

  • School of Architecture and Planning
  • School of Engineering
  • School of Humanities, Arts, and Social Sciences
  • Sloan School of Management
  • School of Science

Admission Requirements

Admission to MIT is highly competitive. Requirements include:

  • MIT Application
  • High school transcript
  • Letters of recommendation
  • SAT or ACT scores (optional)
  • Essays
  • Extracurricular activities

Tuition

For the 2021-2022 academic year:

  • Undergraduate tuition: $53,790
  • Room and board: $17,510
  • Other expenses (books, supplies, etc.): $2,200

Note: MIT meets 100% of demonstrated financial need for admitted students.

Facilities

MIT offers world-class facilities, including:

  • MIT Libraries
  • MIT Museum
  • MIT.nano research center
  • MIT Media Lab
  • Ray and Maria Stata Center
  • Koch Institute for Integrative Cancer Research
