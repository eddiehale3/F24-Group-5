Overview

Stanford University is a private research university located in Stanford, California. Founded in 1885, it is known for its academic excellence, wealth, proximity to Silicon Valley, and ranking as one of the world's top universities.

Location

Stanford University is located at 450 Serra Mall, Stanford, CA 94305, USA. The campus spans 8,180 acres, making it one of the largest in the United States.

Academic Programs

Stanford offers a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs across seven schools:

  • School of Humanities and Sciences
  • School of Engineering
  • School of Medicine
  • School of Law
  • Graduate School of Business
  • Graduate School of Education
  • School of Earth, Energy & Environmental Sciences

Admission Requirements

Admission to Stanford is highly competitive. Requirements include:

  • Common Application or Coalition Application
  • High school transcript
  • Letters of recommendation
  • SAT or ACT scores (optional for 2021-2022)
  • Essays
  • Extracurricular activities

Tuition

For the 2021-2022 academic year:

  • Undergraduate tuition: $55,473
  • Room and board: $17,255
  • Other expenses (books, supplies, etc.): $2,145

Note: Stanford meets 100% of demonstrated financial need for admitted students.

Facilities

Stanford boasts world-class facilities, including:

  • Green Library
  • Cantor Arts Center
  • Stanford Stadium
  • Bing Concert Hall
  • Stanford Linear Accelerator Center (SLAC)
  • Stanford Medical Center
