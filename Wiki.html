Overview

Stanford University is a private research university located in Stanford, California. Founded in 1885, it is known for its academic excellence, wealth, proximity to Silicon Valley, and selectivity in admissions.

Academics

  • 7 schools and 18 interdisciplinary institutes
  • Student-to-faculty ratio: 5:1
  • Notable programs: Computer Science, Engineering, Business

Admissions

Acceptance Rate Average SAT Average ACT
4.3% 1480 33

Campus Life

  • 8,180 acres of campus
  • Over 600 student organizations
  • Strong athletic program (Stanford Cardinal)

Career Prospects

Stanford graduates are highly sought after by top companies and graduate schools. The university has strong connections to Silicon Valley and a vast alumni network.
