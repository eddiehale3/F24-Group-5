Maintaining Your Athletic Scholarship

Eligibility Requirements

To retain your athletic scholarship, you must meet the following requirements:

Minimum GPA

Maintain a minimum grade point average (GPA) of 2.5. This GPA will be calculated at the end of each semester. Failure to meet this requirement may result in the loss of your scholarship.

Credit Hours

Enroll in and successfully complete a minimum of 12 credit hours each semester. This ensures you are making satisfactory academic progress towards your degree. Failure to meet this requirement may result in the loss of your scholarship.

Academic Progress

Maintain satisfactory academic progress as defined by the college's academic progress policy. This may include requirements beyond GPA and credit hours, such as maintaining a certain number of credits earned per attempted credit. Consult your academic advisor or the athletic department for details.

Athletic Participation

Maintain good standing with the athletic team. This includes regular attendance at practices, games, and team events. Specific requirements may vary depending on your sport and the coach's expectations. Consult your coach for details.
